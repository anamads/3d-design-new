Frequently asked questions

  • What is design process like?

    The process is simple and efficient:
    Consultation - We discuss your product, brand, and goals to determine the best creative approach.
    Reference Collection - You provide product images, brand assets (logos, colors, etc.), and any inspiration you like.
    Concept Development - I create a mood board or initial mockups to align with your vision.
    3D Modeling & Texturing - Your product is digitally recreated in high detail.
    Lighting & Rendering - The scene is set up for realistic lighting and camera angles.
    Revisions & Final Delivery - You review and request changes before receiving high-quality final visuals.

  • How long does the work take?

    Project timelines vary based on complexity, but generally:
    Simple product renders (single angle, minimal styling) - up to 7 days
    More complex setups (multiple angles, advanced lighting, stylized compositions) - 1 to 2 weeks
    Custom animations - 2 to 4 weeks
    If you have a deadline, let me know, and I’ll do my best to accommodate!

  • Do you really not need a physical product?

    Yes, that's right! Instead of shipping products, you simply provide clear photos of your product from multiple angles and branding elements (logos, labels, packaging designs). With this information, I can create photorealistic 3D renders without the need for a photoshoot.

  • What if I don’t have a creative strategy or know exactly what I want?

    No problem! I help guide the creative direction based on your brand identity, target audience, and industry trends. I can suggest color schemes, lighting styles, and compositions to match your brand’s aesthetic and marketing goals.

  • What is 3D and how does it work?

    3D visualization/CGI uses digital modeling to create realistic product images and animations. Instead of photographing a physical item, I build a detailed digital version, which allows for unlimited customization, different angles, and perfect lighting—without the limitations of traditional photography.

  • Can you animate my product?

    Yes! 3D animation is a great way to showcase product features, assembly, or functionality in an engaging way. Whether it’s a simple rotating view or a full explainer animation, I can bring your product to life.
