Frequently asked questions

  • What does your process look like?

    The process is simple and efficient:
    Consultation - We discuss your product, brand, and goals to determine the best creative approach.
    Reference Collection - You provide product images, brand assets (logos, colors, etc.), and any inspiration you like.
    Concept Development - I create a mood board or initial mockups to align with your vision.
    3D Modeling & Texturing - Your product is digitally recreated in high detail.
    Lighting & Rendering - The scene is set up for realistic lighting and camera angles.
    Revisions & Final Delivery - You review and request changes before receiving high-quality final visuals.

  • How long does a project take?

    Project timelines vary based on complexity, but generally:
    Simple product renders (single angle, minimal styling) - up to 7 days
    More complex setups (multiple angles, advanced lighting, stylized compositions) - 1 to 2 weeks
    Custom animations - 2 to 4 weeks
    If you have a deadline, let me know, and I’ll do my best to accommodate!

  • Do you need a physical product?

    Yes, instead of shipping products, you simply provide clear photos of your product from multiple angles and branding elements (logos, labels, packaging designs). With this information, I can create photorealistic 3D renders without the need for a photoshoot.

  • What if we don’t have a clear creative direction?

    I help guide the creative direction based on your brand identity, target audience, and industry trends. I can suggest color schemes, lighting styles, and compositions to match your brand’s aesthetic and marketing goals.

  • What types of projects do you typically work on?

    I primarily work on campaign visuals, product launches, and digital experiences for beauty and lifestyle brands.

  • What is the typical investment?

    Projects typically start from $1500, depending on scope and complexity. I'm happy to tailor solutions based on your needs.

  • Do you offer both visuals and interactive experiences?

    Yes, I combine CGI production with interactive design to create engaging digital experiences when needed.
