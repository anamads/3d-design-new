DWB – Dr. Whitney Bowe Beauty | 3D Visuals & Motion for a Science-Driven Skincare Brand.

For DWB, I crafted a high-end series of 3D visuals and animations to elevate their e-commerce presence and social media engagement. My goal was to create a seamless fusion of science, luxury, and storytelling, bringing their latest product launch to life through photorealistic renders and dynamic animations.

GOAL

This project was about more than just aesthetics - it was about translating complex skincare science into engaging, high-quality visuals that resonate with modern consumers. By blending sleek motion design, photorealistic textures, and dynamic storytelling, I helped DWB communicate brand values and product benefits in a way that feels both premium and approachable.
