HAIR HEALTH ESSENTIALS | CGI Images for E-Commerce.

Hair Health Essentials is a wellness-driven haircare brand that launched a dedicated product line to support women experiencing hair changes during menopause. Their focus is on science-backed, nurturing formulas designed to restore strength, volume, and confidence.

GOALS&WORK

The aim was to create clean, modern 3D product visuals that communicate the brand’s care-driven mission. Using only reference photos, I created photorealistic 3D renders for e-commerce and social media. The visuals highlighted key features like packaging design and main ingredients, helping the brand communicate product benefits without costly photoshoots.
