B.TAN | Creative Images for E-Commerce and Social Media.

B.tan is a bold and playful self-tan brand that embraces an unapologetic approach to sunless tanning. Their products focus on effortless application, long-lasting results, and a fun, vibrant brand identity.

GOAL

For this project, I aimed to create high-quality 3D renders that captured the brand’s energetic personality while showcasing the products in an engaging, visually appealing way. Through realistic textures, dynamic compositions, and striking lighting, I aimed to elevate the brand’s digital presence and provide compelling visuals for marketing and e-commerce.
