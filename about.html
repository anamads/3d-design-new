About Me.

I’m Anastasia Orlova, a 3D artist and creative developer working with beauty and lifestyle brands to create high-end CGI visuals and digital experiences.

Based in Bangkok, I specialize in product-focused visuals — from photorealistic renders and motion to interactive web experiences. My work combines technical precision with a strong focus on composition, materials, and brand alignment. I approach each project from both a creative and strategic perspective, ensuring the final visuals not only look refined, but also support campaigns, launches, and digital platforms. When needed, I collaborate with other creatives and developers to deliver complete, high-quality outcomes.
