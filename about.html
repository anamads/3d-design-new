HEY THERE, YOU FOUND ME.

I'm Anastasia Orlova, a passionate 3D artist and creative developer specializing in product design, CGI, and motion graphics.

Based in Bangkok, I combine my skills in product design, UX research, graphic design, and marketing to create captivating visual experiences. I believe in the power of visuals and always strive to bring ideas to life. I focus on creating immersive experiences through realistic product visualizations and dynamic motion graphics. I understand the importance of effective visual communication and aim to go beyond just aesthetics, crafting designs that convey a compelling story and resonate with audiences.

Collaboration is at the core of my creative process. I love working alongside visionary clients, talented designers, and cross-functional teams to bring ideas to fruition. I believe in a collaborative environment that can unlock the true potential of every project and create exceptional visual solutions with a lasting impact.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. If you have any questions or just want to say hi, don't hesitate to get in touch. I look forward to connecting with you!
