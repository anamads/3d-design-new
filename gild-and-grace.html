GILD&GRACE | CGI Images for E-Commerce.

Gild&Grace is a modern online jewelry brand known for elegant, timeless pieces. They use high-quality 3D renders to showcase their products without the need for physical shipping, making their content production faster and more efficient.

GOAL

To create photorealistic visuals and animations highlighting the craftsmanship and shine of Gild&Grace’s jewelry, helping boost online engagement and drive e-commerce sales. I developed a series of 3D images and short animations based on reference photos, tailored for their website and social media. The renders delivered consistent, high-end visuals that matched their branding and elevated their digital presence.
